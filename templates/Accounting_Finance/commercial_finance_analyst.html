Commercial Finance Analyst

Our client, has an unrivalled opportunity for a commercially astute part qualified accountant to join their highly sought after, talent scheme based at their hugely impressive UK Headquarters in Bedfordshire. The successful applicant will gain exposure working in a true finance business partner capacity, working with nonfinance stakeholders. You will be required to liaise, influence and build relationships at director level and will gain unparalleled exposure in this capacity. Responsibilities will include: development of retail pricing and ensuring the process is coordinated with all key stakeholders; developing effective pricing strategies to support retail stores and develop optimum selling price; working with commercial teams to understand customer profiles to make accurate decisions within the pricing proposal; producing and presenting at senior management / director level using PowerPoint to clearly demonstrate your findings and to provide reassurance that pricing proposals are financially sound and accurate. The successful candidate will ideally be of graduate calibre, CIMA / ACCA part qualified, with an excellent record of achievement. Your experience to date will be gained working with a large company environment. You will be an outgoing and confident individual, a first class communicator and have the ambition to reach Senior Manager level. The successful candidate will be rewarded with excellent benefits, onsite restaurant, superb social and out of work activities and the tools and training to succeed at the highest level. Where UK qualifications are specified we will take into account overseas equivalents. To apply for this position candidates must be eligible to work in country in which the role is based.
